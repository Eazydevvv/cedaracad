About Us

Cedar Academic Services is an academic and research services organization. While CedarAcad started formally in 2023, work started in 2020 with research writing, editing and bioinformatics analysis. The focus of this organization are disciplines in biomedical, life and social sciences. With working partners and mentors across many disciplines, we offer various services including research assistance, statistical and genomics data analysis.

Our Services

Genomics:

We carry out NGS, metagenomics and other genomic analysis for students and researchers.

Language Editing:

Our professional and vastly experienced English language experts will proofread and edit your academic document, after which an editorial certificate will be issued to you.

Research assistance:

We support with literature reviews, data collection, and research design.

Statistical analysis:

We assist with statistical analysis, interpretation, and visualization.

Formatting and publication support:

We help with formatting academic papers for publication.

Academic Integrity|Plagiarism Services:

Using Turnitin, your document is scanned for plagiarism, and our staff paraphrases for you till an acceptable limit is reached. We also offer support for proper citation.
